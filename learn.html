🧠 Learn

Here are some disasters and what you should do:

  • 🌊 Food: Move to higher ground, never play in flood water.
  • 🔥 Fire: Stop, drop, and roll if your clothes catch fire.
  • 🌍 Earthquake: Drop, cover, and hold on under a table.
  • ⛈️ Storm: Stay indoors and away from windows.
  • 🚨 Emergency: Always call an adult or dial 112 in India.
