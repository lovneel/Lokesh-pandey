🔥 Fire Safety

If your clothes catch fire — STOP, DROP, and ROLL! Stay low if there is smoke and get outside quickly.

  • 🧯 **Never play with matches or lighters.**
  • 🏠 **Know the exit route** in your house in case of fire.
  • 🛑 **Stop, Drop, and Roll** if your clothes catch fire.
  • 📞 **Call adults or emergency services** immediately if you see fire.
  • 🚪 **Stay low under smoke** — smoke rises, so crawl to escape.
  • 🔥 **Keep flammable objects away** from heat or fire.
  • 🚫 **Do not hide during a fire** — get out quickly and safely.
  • 🛡️ **Practice fire drills** with your family regularly.
  • 💧 **If safe, use water or fire extinguisher** to put small fires out.
