🍎 Food Safety Tips

Eating safe food keeps you strong and healthy! Here are some simple rules:

  • 🧼 **Wash hands** before eating.
  • 🥦 **Eat fresh fruits and vegetables.**
  • 🍶 **Drink clean water** — avoid dirty or uncovered water.
  • 🥪 **Don't eat food that has fallen on the ground.**
  • 🍔 **Avoid junk food** every day — eat it only sometimes.
  • 🧊 **Keep food covered** to protect from flies.
  • 🗑️ **Throw spoiled food away** — don’t taste it!
